Recommended Maintenance Intervals
Standard Recommendations:
  • Commercial UAVs: Every 25-50 flight hours or 3-6 months
  • Professional UAVs: Every 50-100 flight hours or 6-12 months
  • Industrial UAVs: Every 15-25 flight hours or 2-3 months
  • Recreational UAVs: Every 100+ flight hours or 12+ months
Factors to Consider:
  • Operating environment (dusty, humid, etc.)
  • Flight patterns (aggressive vs. gentle)
  • Payload weight and usage
  • Weather conditions
  • Manufacturer recommendations
